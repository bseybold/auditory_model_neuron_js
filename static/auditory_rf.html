A Model Auditory Neuron

This page is designed to allow you to see how spectrotemporal receptive fields shape responses of auditory neurons.

The colored squares below represent a spectrotemporal receptive field. The x-axis represents 5ms time bins from the current stimulus timepoint. The y-axis represents different spectral (frequency) bands. I have arbitrarily chosen frequencies that respond well to human speech. By clicking on one of the squares in the receptive field, the value will change between exciatory (red), inhibitory (blue), and neutral (grey).

Once you have designed the receptive field to your liking, record or submit a sound file (.wav) and upload them both to the server. The server will estimate the response of this filter to the stimulus you record.
